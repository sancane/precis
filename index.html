About PRECIS

The PRECIS (Preparation, Enforcement, and Comparison of Internationalized Strings) Framework defines how to handle internationalized strings in application protocols, with special attention to security and usability concerns.

  • Preparation: Validate that input characters are allowed
  • Enforcement: Apply normalization, mapping, and trimming rules
  • Comparison: Determine if two strings are equivalent
